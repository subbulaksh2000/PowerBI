## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyses pharmacy operations across multiple countries to uncover key drivers of **revenue, profitability, and regional performance**.

The dashboard provides a **data-driven and interactive view** of business performance, enabling stakeholders to make informed decisions on **market strategy, operational efficiency, and growth opportunities**.

---

## 🎯 Key Business Question

**How do revenue and profitability vary across countries, regions, and pharmacies, and which areas contribute most to overall business performance?**

---

## 📊 Key Metrics

- 💰 **Total Revenue:** €8.63M  
- 📈 **Total Margin:** €2.42M  
- 📦 **Total Units Sold:** 445.79K  
- 📊 **Margin %:** 28.0%  

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### 🌍 Revenue by Country
- Identifies top-performing markets  
- Germany leads total revenue contribution  
- Highlights variation across countries  

---

### 📅 Revenue Trend Over Time
- Shows consistent growth from 2024 to 2025  
- Helps identify performance trends  

---

### 🧮 Profitability Analysis
- Margin and margin % highlight operational efficiency  
- Enables comparison between high-revenue and high-profit markets  

---

### 🧩 Country Contribution (Treemap)
- Visualises proportional contribution of each country  
- Helps identify dominant and underperforming markets  

---

### 📊 Revenue vs Profitability (Scatter Plot)
- Compares **Total Revenue vs Total Margin**  
- Identifies:
  - High revenue but low profit countries  
  - Efficient high-margin markets  

---

### 🎛️ Interactive Filters
- Country  
- Region  
- Pharmacy Type  
- Promotion Flag  

Allows dynamic and flexible data exploration.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Germany is the leading contributor to total revenue  
- Revenue shows a steady upward trend  
- Some countries generate high sales but lower margins → potential inefficiencies  
- Regional performance varies significantly, indicating optimisation opportunities  
- Efficiency differences identified through revenue vs margin comparison
